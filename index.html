About John

Hello! I'm John, a certified swimming and gymnastics instructor with many years of experience teaching children of all ages and skill levels.

My passion is helping young learners build confidence, develop essential water and movement skills, and most importantly, have fun while learning. I believe every child can succeed with the right instruction and encouragement.

I teach at my gym or pool or yours, ensuring flexibility and convenience for busy families.
